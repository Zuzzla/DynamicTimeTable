Why DynamicTimeTable?

Fixed timetables lock each train to one destination. DynamicTimeTable lets equivalent stations share one destination group and selects a suitable station when routing is processed. Vanilla timetable settings remain unchanged; only the next destination may be exchanged.

FIXED ROUTING

One train, one locked destination

DYNAMIC GROUP ROUTING
